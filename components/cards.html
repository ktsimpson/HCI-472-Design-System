Cards

The following components are used for an introductory statement on the landing page, external social media navigation, and the site's navigation bar.

For All

This is the card component used for all screens (desktop, tablet, and mobile).


Usage & Best Practices

Cards should be used sparingly. They are best used when giving a preview of longer form content that requires an additional page (see the use of cards on the portfolio vs. case studies page). The cards in this system are meant to use images as a background, which is recommended but not necessary to following for this system.

Accessibility

They should be styled with a light shadow so they stand out from the page, and ensure that they are large enough to hold legible text for readers.

            <div class="card" style="background-color: black;">
