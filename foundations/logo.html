Logo

The logo is meant to incorporate the initials of the moniker I often go by online, KT Simpson. The T acts as both the middle initial of the moniker, as well as the handle of the simplified umbrella propped above the initials. I wanted to include an umbrella in the logo as I find them cute and whimsical, as well as evoking a sense of safety or protection. The following logo design variations include: main color, pastel version, black and white, reverse, and favicon.

Main Color Logo

This (minimal) color logo is used to emphasize the K and S, as well as the shape of the umbrella. The off-white on near-black background evokes a clean approach to design, while the pink heightens the whimsical nature of the umbrella.


Pastel Color Logo

This alternate color logo uses pastel colors. It can be used on both light and dark backgrounds.


Black & White

This black and white logo simplifies the color logo and makes it more ideal for modern design or grayscale. It would best be used on a white background.


Black & White Reverse

This variant of the logo is an inverse of the black and white logo. It should be used on dark backgrounds.

Favicon

This is the favicon used for quick identification of the webpage. It uses a black K in the main body font, Arimo, on the pink (#febdbd) background for easy use on both light and dark browsers.
