Typography

Alice & Arimo

The typography used in this design system aids in achieving the balance between whimsicality and minimalism I sought in creating my portfolio. Alice evokes a fantastical sense with its heavier weight and flourishing serifs. Arimo complements Alice as a lighter, sans-serif typeface, perfect for accessible body text. The typographical hierarchy is based on a perfect fourth
