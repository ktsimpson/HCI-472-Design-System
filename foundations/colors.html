Colors

There are five colors used in this design system. The color scheme is almost monochromatic, save for the inclusion of the medium-light pink. The mostly monochromatic palette, again, lends to a sense of clean, minimalist design, while the addition of pink for details within the design system adds a flair of femininity.

#f3f3f3

This color is used for all body text on the portfolio site, as well as some headers. It is also used for the social media icons and hamburger menu toggle.


#fedbdb

This color is used in the logo, buttons, favicon, and for some headers. It is also used for the decorative bars seen in the page patterns.


#ababab

This color is used in the navigation menu and in some body text.


#000000 (60% opacity)

This color is used for the cards. It is the darkest black at 60% opacity.


#202022

This color is used for the background of the entire portfolio site, as well as for any dark text that appears on a lighter background, such as a button.
